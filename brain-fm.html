Tested on Brain and Behavior
Scientists at Brain.fm work with collaborators at academic institutions to run experiments.
We look at the effects of our technology on the brain, using fMRI and EEG, and also run large-scale behavioral tests, measuring perfomance on simple games.
Our experiments always include a control condition ('placedo') of the same underlying music without Brain.fm technology applied, so we can be sure our tech is what makes the difference.
